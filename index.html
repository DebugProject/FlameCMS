Twitter

Follow us on Twitter and be the first to receive news and updates. We are open for your feedback and questions.

Contribute on GitHub

You want to take part in the development? Fork UIkit on GitHub and send us a pull request. To report a bug, file a new issue.

Facebook Community

If you're interested in discussing, participating and helping each other, the Facebook FlameCMS community is the place to be.

Open Source

FlameCMS is an open source CMS for private servers.
Create your own styles and discover the unlimited use of UIkit.

MIT License

FlameCMS is absolutely free of charge and you can use, copy, merge, publish and distribute the framework without any limitations.

Our Heros

A big thanks to our developers and contributors. FlameCMS wouldn't be possible without these awesome open source projects.

Active Development

FlameCMS build it all, using our extensive experience in creating Joomla, WordPress, PrestaShop and Drupal themes and applications in codeigniter, to power the CMS.
